The services it offers

Sensing of Motion

The scarecrow is equipped with a motion sensor that detects movement . When an intruder(animal or bird) is detected, the scarecrow activates its alarm system.

Soil Monitoring

Measuring the soil composition such as nitrogen, phosphorus , potassium etc. Based on soil condition, suggestion of crops to be grown to maximize the income.

Crop Prediction

The scarecrow is equipped with a soil monitoring system that detects the soil condition. It suggests the crops that can be grown in the soil.

Animal detection

The scarecrow is equipped with a camera and ML model that detects the animals and birds. The data of all the previous attacks is stored in the database.
